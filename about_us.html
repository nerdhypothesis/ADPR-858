About Us

Their News is a first of its kind aggregating and crowdsourcing local news website for racially diverse minority communities in Nebraska and bordering states of Colorado, Iowa, Kansas, Missouri, South Dakota and Wyoming.

We aim to increase racial diversity in news coverage, encourage crowdsourcing of news from minority neighborhoods and ggregate racially diverse news for higher circulation to a focused audiences

Send us your original reporting at write2theirnews@gmail.com
