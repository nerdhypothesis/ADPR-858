About Us

THEIR News is the first of its kind local news website in Midwest USA. We aim to increase news coverage on racially diverse minority communities through original reporting, crowdsourcing and aggregating.

At present we are covering communities in Nebraska and bordering states of Colorado, Iowa, Kansas, Missouri, South Dakota and Wyoming. If you are a resident in these states you can send us your articles, videos or photo stories based on local news reporting in your region or opinion.
